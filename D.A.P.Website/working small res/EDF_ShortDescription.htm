One of the aims of the SERIES project is to improve dissemination and use of experimental results, to foster the impact of earthquake engineering research on practice, innovation and earthquake risk mitigation and to provide a broad and solid base for the calibration of numerical models. This requires harmonization and unification of database activities and the possibility of accessing data through a unique portal, by providing a centralized access to different database nodes distributed over the network and able to communicate with a central portal in a uniform manner.
